## Skilly Beta — $19/month

Only tier available during the beta. Annual billing launches post-beta.

### Price
- **$19 / month** (USD)
- Monthly billing only (no annual option during beta)
- Per-day equivalent: ~$0.63/day
- Billed via Polar (polar.sh)

### What's included
- 3 hours of live AI tutoring per month
- Works with any macOS app on your Mac (no skill required for basic use)
- All 5 bundled example skills: Blender Fundamentals, After Effects Basics, Premiere Pro Basics, DaVinci Resolve Basics, Figma Basics
- 16 languages with auto-detection
- 8 AI voices (Alloy, Echo, Fable, Onyx, Nova, Shimmer, Sage, Breeze)
- Multi-monitor support — works across all connected displays
- Live Tutor mode — hands-free voice activation (no hotkey required)
- Push-to-talk via customizable keyboard shortcut (default: Control+Option)
- Custom skills — drop in your own SKILL.md files
- Auto-activates the matching skill when you switch apps
- Auto-updates via Sparkle framework

### Free trial
- **15 minutes free on first launch** — no credit card required
- One-shot lifetime trial per user (doesn't reset)
- No checkout before the trial; download and start using immediately

### Cancellation and guarantees
- Cancel anytime from inside the app (Settings → Account → Manage subscription)
- Keep access through the end of your billing period after canceling
- **Beta price is locked for life on your account** — when the beta ends and prices change, beta users keep $19/month forever

### Limits
- Beta is capped at the first 50 users
- 3 hours of usage per month (cap resets on the billing period boundary, not a rolling window)
- 80% warning notification at 2h 24m used

## Platform

- macOS 14.0 or later
- Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4) and Intel Macs supported
- ~2 MB app size

## Platforms NOT yet supported (waitlist only)
- Windows — in development, no ETA
- Linux — on the roadmap
- iOS / iPadOS — exploring

To join the waitlist for non-macOS platforms, visit the [Waitlist section](https://tryskilly.app/#waitlist) on the homepage.

## Post-beta pricing

When the beta ends, Skilly will ladder up to multiple tiers based on real usage data. Beta users will keep the $19/month rate forever on their existing accounts.
